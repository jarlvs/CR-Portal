ABOUT US

Abhyuday, IIT Bombay is a student run organization working towards creating a sense of Social Responsibility and Leadership among the youth of India.


College Representative Programme (CR) is an initiative to form a team of spirited and self-motivated college students from campuses pan-India. These enthusiastic individuals will demonstrate leadership to spread the social awareness amongst their peers, their batchmates and in their college, thus bringing all like-minded individuals under one banner . Abhyuday, IIT Bombay, in conjunction with these students wish to bring a change we all wish to herald in the society.




WHY COLLEGE REPRESENTATIVE?

Management

Improve your social and management skills to organise social events and launch campaigns in your college in collaboration with Abhyuday.

Communication

An opportunity to enhance communication skills as you get to interact with other College Representatives and students from your’s and other colleges.

Prizes

A chance to receive a certificate of appreciation for being an integral part of Abhyuday 2016, IIT Bombay as well as various exciting prizes worth INR 50 K.



Responsibilities as a CR

Social Media

Publicising our events on various social media platforms by sharing our posts in your college groups etc.

On Ground Publicity

Putting up posters of Abhyuday on your respective college boards within 4 days of receiving it.

Coordination

Coordinating with the Abhyuday team and helping organise Multicity Campaigns




Terms & conditions
